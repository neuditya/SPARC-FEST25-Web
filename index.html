About 
SPARC FEST '25

See More

SPARC FEST 2025 is a one-of-a-kind hardware hackathon that serves as a vital platform for innovation, bridging the gap between classroom theory and real-world engineering for young minds. As the first-ever high school level hardware hackathon in Nepal, the event brought together over 60 students from 13 different schools to explore their interests in depth through hands-on competition.

Participants were challenged to develop physical solutions for some of the world's most pressing issues, focusing on themes such as disaster management, assisting the disabled, environmental sustainability, urban waste management, and healthcare. By providing a stage for future innovators to collaborate and compete, SPARC FEST 2025 stands as an impactful gathering where ambitious ideas are transformed into tangible hardware prototypes for social good.
