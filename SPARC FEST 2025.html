About 
SPARC FEST '25

See More

SPARC FEST 2025 is a one-of-a-kind hardware hackathon that serves as a vital platform for innovation, bridging the gap between classroom theory and real-world engineering for young minds. As the first-ever high school level hardware hackathon in Nepal, the event brought together over 60 students from 13 different schools to explore their interests in depth through hands-on competition.

Participants were challenged to develop physical solutions for some of the world's most pressing issues, focusing on themes such as disaster management, assisting the disabled, environmental sustainability, urban waste management, and healthcare. By providing a stage for future innovators to collaborate and compete, SPARC FEST 2025 stands as an impactful gathering where ambitious ideas are transformed into tangible hardware prototypes for social good.

SPARC FEST Event Timeline

EVENT HIGHLIGHTS:

9:00 A.M. — Grand Kick-off & The Robotic Unveiling

The hackathon officially commenced with a prestigious opening ceremony. Following the traditional lighting of the Panas and a celebratory ribbon-cutting, the atmosphere electrified as the final countdown reached zero. With the competition formally underway, the specialized robotics packages were unboxed, marking the transition from conceptual planning to hands-on engineering

12:01 P.M. — Innovation in Progress

By midday, the venue was a hive of activity. Participants were fully immersed in the "build phase," deeply engaged in the iterative process of assembly and troubleshooting. The energy remained high as teams collaborated intensely, turning raw components into functional prototypes while navigating the technical challenges of their specific builds..

02:30 P.M. — Pitching & Technical Jury

The focus shifted from the workbench to the stage as the Presentation Phase began. Each team stepped forward to pitch their unique concepts and demonstrate their hardware to a distinguished panel of judges. These sessions tested the participants' ability to articulate their logic, defend their design choices, and showcase the real-world viability of their robotic solutions.

5:50 P.M. — Grand Finale & Awards Ceremony

As the clock struck five, the tools were downed and the hackathon officially concluded. After a rigorous deliberation by the judges, the winners were declared during the closing ceremony. The event ended on a high note with the distribution of prizes, celebrating the exceptional ingenuity, teamwork, and technical prowess displayed throughout the day

The winners

 Winners of SPARC FEST '25

One outstanding team  was selected from each of the three themes and honored with a prize of NPR 10,000, along with medals and certificates. Teams were evaluated based on technical expertise, practicality, level of sophistication of the project, quality of presentation, and the final outcome, recognizing excellence and innovation.
